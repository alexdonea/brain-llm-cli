# Bibliography & Source Index

> This is the deduplicated master index across the five `docs/research/` reports. Every entry below
> was drawn from those reports - **no new sources were invented**, and where a report cited a work with
> only author + year (no recoverable title), that is preserved verbatim rather than fabricating one. A
> work cited in several reports appears here exactly **once**, with its variants merged. The project's
> honesty line holds throughout: **we model the function, never the feeling** - every quantity is a
> functional/behavioral correlate, never a claim of phenomenal experience or sentience.
